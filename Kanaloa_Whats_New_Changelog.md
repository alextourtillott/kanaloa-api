# What’s New with Kanaloa: Ocean Restoration Changelog

Welcome to the Kanaloa changelog! We're moving fast to build the tools you need to restore our oceans. Based on your field feedback, our team ships weekly updates to make your data crisp and your autonomous missions smooth.

Here's a look at what we recently launched to supercharge your restoration projects!

---

## June 2026 Updates

### June 15, 2026 — Smart Forecasting & Speedier Exports

* **New Feature: AI-Driven Growth Horizons:** You asked for deeper predictive insights, and we delivered! When creating a Data Report, you can now toggle **"Smart Forecasting"** to view 3-month and 6-month biological projections based on active reef data.
* **⚡ Performance Boost: 4x Faster CSV Compilations:** Large datasets containing months of raw telemetry caused sluggish downloads. We re-architected our reporting engine so you can export massive ZIP archives in seconds, keeping your workflows unblocked.
* **Bug Fix:** Resolved a minor timezone mapping issue where some AUV logs recorded in UTC were offset on local dashboard timelines.

---

## May 2026 Updates

### May 28, 2026 — Dynamic Sandbox & Deeper Species Tracking

**New Feature: Interactive API Sandbox:** For our Marine Robotics Engineers, integrating with Kanaloa is now effortless. Our API Reference docs now feature a live, interactive test sandbox. You can modify payloads and simulate requests directly from the browser before deploying code to your hardware!
**Expanded Species Library:** Added support for tracking five additional macroalgae and cold-water coral species requested by teams operating in temperate marine zones.
**Bug Fix:** Fixed a UI glitch in the Dashboard sidebar where site connection statuses occasionally failed to refresh after a drone completed its upload.

### May 12, 2026 — Mobile-Friendly Maps & Extended Battery Metrics

**New Feature: Responsive Site Maps:** Sailing on a boat or checking data on a tablet? The **Restoration Sites** map view is now fully responsive, making it much easier to pinch, zoom, and inspect drone flight paths on mobile devices.
**Advanced Telemetry Blocks:** AUV logs now explicitly track battery efficiency degradation patterns over time, helping you predict maintenance windows before your equipment hits the water.
